Alexandra Pike

I'm a Postdoctoral research in the Institute of Cognitive Neuroscience. I am part of the Neuroscience and Mental Health Group, and I work with Dr Oliver Robinson.

I did my DPhil at the Department of Psychiatry at the University of Oxford. I'm currently working on eating disorders and compulsivity. I'm doing some MRI work, some psychopharmacological work and also learning some computational psychiatry. My DPhil supervisors were Professor Philip Cowen and Professor Rebecca Park.

I have previously completed an MSc in Neuroscience and a BA in Psychology and Philosophy.
